## 🌱 What Is Custom Landscape Design?

Custom landscape design is a **tailored approach to outdoor planning** that focuses on your unique property, needs, and lifestyle. Instead of using generic templates, professional designers create a plan specifically for you.

### Key Elements of Custom Design:
- Site analysis (soil, sunlight, drainage)
- Personalized layout planning
- Plant and material selection
- Hardscape integration (patios, walkways, walls)
- Long-term sustainability

In Dix Hills, where homes often have spacious yards, custom landscaping allows homeowners to fully utilize their outdoor areas in creative and practical ways.
